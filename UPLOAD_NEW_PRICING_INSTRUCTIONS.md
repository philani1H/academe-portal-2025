# How to Upload the New Pricing Data

## Quick Steps

1. **Login to Admin Dashboard**
   - Go to your admin panel
   - Navigate to **Content Management**

2. **Go to Pricing Section**
   - Click on the **Pricing** tab

3. **Upload the New Data**
   - Click the **"Bulk Upload"** button
   - Choose either file:
     - `new-pricing-data.json` (recommended)
     - `new-pricing-data.csv`
   - Click to upload

4. **Verify**
   - You should see a success message
   - The pricing plans will be updated/created automatically

## What Will Happen

The system will:
- Update existing plans if names match
- Create new plans if they don't exist
- Show you how many were updated vs created
